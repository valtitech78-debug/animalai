Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 June 2026

Animal AI (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is an independent app developed by Massimiliano Merlo, an individual developer based in Italy. This policy explains what data the app handles, why, and the choices you have. We designed Animal AI to collect as little as possible.

1. Information we handle

  • Photos you scan. When you identify an animal, the image is sent to our AI provider to generate the identification. Photos are used only to perform that identification.
  • Your collection. The species cards and achievements you unlock are stored to keep your collection across sessions.
  • Purchase status. If you subscribe to Pro, Apple tells the app whether your subscription is active. We never see your card details or payment information — Apple handles all billing.
  • Basic technical data. Limited, non-identifying technical information may be processed to keep the service running and diagnose errors.

You can use Animal AI’s free scans without creating an account. We do not ask for your name, email or phone number to use the app. If you email us for support, we’ll have whatever you choose to write to us.

2. How we use it

  • To identify the animal in your photo and show you its profile, confidence score and rarity.
  • To maintain your collection, achievements and progress.
  • To process and restore in-app purchases through Apple.
  • To operate, secure and improve the app.

3. Service providers

Animal AI relies on a small number of trusted providers to function. We do not sell your data to anyone.

  • Anthropic (Claude API). Your scanned photo is sent to Anthropic’s Claude vision model to perform the identification. Per Anthropic’s commercial terms, inputs sent through the API are not used to train its models. See anthropic.com for details.
  • Supabase. Provides the backend (database and edge functions) that powers the app’s requests.
  • Apple. Distributes the app and processes all subscription payments through the App Store.

4. Retention

Scanned images are transmitted only to carry out the identification and are not kept by us to build advertising or training datasets. Your collection data is retained so your progress persists; you can clear your collection at any time from within the app.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object to processing or request portability. Because the app works without an account, we often hold no information that identifies you personally. To make a request, email valtitech78@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6. Children

Animal AI is a general-audience app and is not directed at children under the age required for consent in their country. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email valtitech78@gmail.com.
